Of all the pieces I completed, this one took me by far the least time. I started it in the evening on one schoolday in late January, and finished it the next day.

Before I started writing it, I had been contemplating one of the main themes of the piano concerto I had been working on.

I was meticulously looping each measure individually to see if I could identify certain notes I wanted to change. And as a result, I unearthed this melody.

I quite liked how it sounded repeated over and over, so I created a new score and essentially pasted the same thing for 116 measures. Then I added a simple baseline and a few variations and ended up with this.
